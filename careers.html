Corporate Partnerships ( Acquisitions ) 

 Work Locations – Bangalore, Gurgaon, Hyderabad, Mumbai, Kolkata
 Exp – 5+Yrs

 Job Description :-
• Create & Maintain Database of potential donors
• Raise CSR funding from large corporate & SME, International foundations and multilateral aid agencies
• Achieve annual Individual target of new grants each year
• Represent organization at forums, events etc
• Plan & Execute Employee Giving Programs, Cause Related Marketing Campaigns

  Must - have :-
• 5+ years of experience in B2B sales with at least 1 year of CSR (including cold-calling, seeking appointments and doing meetings, proposal writing, finalizing contracts)
• Fluent verbal and written communications in English with excellent Power Point & Excel Skills
• Great people skills – to create new and maintain existing relationships
• Must have a passion to work in the development sector with a flair for extensive networking 
• Any Graduate/Post-Graduate degree in Business Management or Marketing will be preferred

Corporate Partnerships – ( Relationships ) 

   Work Locations – Bangalore, Gurgaon, Hyderabad, Mumbai

  Job Description - 
• Single point of contact for existing Corporate Donors to service their requirements
• Ensure MoU/Contract compliance by coordinating with internal stakeholders 
• Track project execution and facilitate field visits 
• Create periodic reports – quarterly and annual to apprise donor of key development
• Strengthen partnership through leadership engagement, employee volunteering, donor engagement events
• Ensure renewal & enhancement of donor relationships with individual renewal targets.

  Must-have:
• 10+ years of experience in Sales, Relationship Management with at least 2 year of CSR experience (including proposal/report writing, finalizing contracts, CRM campaigns)
• Fluent verbal and written communications in English with excellent Power Point & Excel Skills to confidently engage & present to CXOs, Board Members
• Thorough understanding of CSR Act, Development Sector
• Acquaintance with Impact Evaluation Frameworks – Theory of Change, Output-Outcome-Impact Tracks, Logframe Analysis

Monitoring & Evaluation 

 Work Locations – Yeshwanthpur, Bangalore

Job Description - 

• Conceptualise and anchor research on the impact areas, relevant to work of Akshaya Patra. 
• Lead engagement with 3rd party research, impact assessment agencies, research institutions to drive on-field research
• Formulate scope and nature of different studies to be carried to substantiate the work and outcome of Akshaya Patra
• Monitor and ensure the quality of the field work and data management during the implementation phase, and the quality of the analysis and ease of understanding during the report writing phase
• Communicate results of research projects to internal and external constituents through a variety of avenues (research reports, convening, trainings, conference presentations, web resources, etc.)
• Serve as the coordinator for local participation in state wide or national initiatives (Health & Economic Prosperity Reports, etc.)
• Ensure that the monitoring and evaluation capacities within local operations of Akshaya Patra are strengthened enabling them to increasingly engage in and lead monitoring and evaluation processes.
• Promote the awareness and understanding of the shared responsibility of M&E function among all staff members through communication, training, learning and development activities organization-wide.
• Education - Post Graduate Degree in Social Sciences, Statistics, Social Work.

 Experience & Skills 
• 3+ Years of experience in social sector
• Demonstrated expertise in operations research, impact evaluation, and monitoring and evaluation of nutrition and other public health programs
• Prior experience of working with field research agencies
• Effective Project Management skills
• Excellent Communication & analytical skills
• Stakeholder engagement skills

Please share your resume to [email protected]
